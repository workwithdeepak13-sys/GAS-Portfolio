About this System

1 · What This System Is

This is a Production Planning & Shop-Floor Execution System. It replaces the planner's personal Excel, the whiteboard, and the 45-minute "as per my data" morning argument with a single source of truth: what was planned per machine per shift, what was actually produced hour by hour, which machine is down and why, and whether today's dispatch commitment is safe. Its single most important outcome is a morning meeting that starts from agreed numbers.

Built for: Planner, Section Head, Operator, Plant Head, Admin.

2 · The Problem It Solves

  • Three versions of the truth (planning Excel, whiteboard, verbal) — 45 min/day of meeting argument ≈ 190 hours/year of senior time.
  • Downtime unrecorded — a 90-minute breakdown that nobody times costs the shift's plan silently.
  • Dispatch risk discovered by the customer — penalty clauses and expedited freight.
  • No OEE — capacity investment decisions made on gut feel.
  • Changeover waste invisible — poor sequencing burns 30+ min per SKU switch.
  • Late/backfilled entries indistinguishable from live data — accountability impossible.

7 · Assumptions & Limits

  • Single plant, single currency; shift A assumed 06:00–14:00 for plan math (Shifts sheet drives display).
  • Practical ceiling ≈ 30 concurrent users; ~50k ProductionEntries rows before dashboard slows — archive quarterly.
  • Apps Script quotas: 6-min execution cap per run, ~100 MailApp recipients/day (consumer) — digests are batched.
  • No true real-time push — plant board polls every 60 seconds.
  • Deliberately does NOT do: finite-capacity scheduling optimization, machine-PLC integration, multi-plant rollup. Upgrade path: export sheets to BigQuery + keep this app as the capture layer.
