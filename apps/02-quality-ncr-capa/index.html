About this System

1 · What This System Is

A Quality Inspection, NCR & CAPA Management System for in-plant quality: incoming, in-process, and final inspection with enforced non-conformance and corrective-action workflows. It replaces paper inspection registers, WhatsApp rejection photos, and verbal root-cause analysis. Its single most important outcome is that the same defect stops returning every quarter — because repeat defects mechanically force a verified CAPA.

Built for: Quality Head, Inspector, Section Head, Plant Head, Admin.

2 · The Problem It Solves

  • Paper inspection registers — 3 panic-days of reconstruction per customer audit.
  • Rejection notes as WhatsApp photos — no disposition authority, no traceability.
  • Root cause never verified — the same defect returns quarterly, each recurrence costing rework + rejection value.
  • No answer to "which supplier / machine / defect costs us the most" — quality spend is blind.
  • "Use-as-is" decisions made informally — audit findings and customer escapes.
  • CAPA closure without effectiveness check — corrective actions that never corrected anything.

7 · Assumptions & Limits

  • Single plant; COPQ rates are flat per-unit (Config) — not item-specific costing.
  • Photos referenced by note text; Drive-file attachment is a phase-2 item.
  • ~20k Inspections rows before register screens need archiving; practical ceiling ≈ 30 concurrent users.
  • MailApp daily quota applies to escalations; digests batch all items into one email.
  • Deliberately NOT: customer complaints (internal quality only), SPC control charts, gauge R&R. Upgrade path: export to a stats package; this app remains the capture and workflow layer.
